Jawa's Story

Jawa is just a random PC building I just created from my mind. Let's say it is found at a mall named Al Boustan in Cairo, Egypt, where are all types of people to meet, including Scammers, and I have a story about that. One time my laptop's motherboard was fried While I was doing some Arduino project, and as any normal Egyptian I went to Al Boustan to find what the problem is. As before I arrived there, I didn't know what the problem was. After going to a store there he replaced my motherboard for 200$ which is so expensive only to be cooked again for no reason in the next day. However, he replaced it again for free, but I know for sure that this isn't a fresh new motherboard made by Dell as the seller is marketing. When I thought for name suitable to be a name for a store there, I thought of Jawa, why? IDK.
